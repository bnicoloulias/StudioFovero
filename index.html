About Studio Fovero

In August 2001, we opened Studio Fovero Salon and Spa. It was a big, crazy thing to do, but we weren't afraid... maybe we should've been. We started small, with a handful of clients and a dedicated staff of stylists. Today we have a total of 11 stylists and spa specialists working hard to make sure all of our friends and customers all look ultra-foxy all the time. If you're already our customer, you know we love you, right?!? And if you haven't yet come in, why not call today? We're always looking to make new friends.......
